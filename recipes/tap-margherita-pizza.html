Tap Margherita Pizza Recipe

    ingredients list

  • Pizza dough
  • Tomatoes sauce
  • Mussarela chesee
  • Fresh basil
  • Olive oil
  • Fresh tomatoes

    Directions

  1. Pre heat the oven at 180C
  2. Cover the pizza dough with the tomatoes sauce
  3. With your hands cut some basil leaves
  4. Cover the pizza with Mussarela chesee
  5. Add some tomatoes slices over the chesee
  6. Bring the pizza into the oven for 15min or till melt the chesee
  7. Take out the pizza from the oven and add few more basil leaves on the top
  8. Add a generous drizzle of olive oil over the pizza
  9. Cut the pizza in 4 or 8 slices
  10. Now you just need to tasty your flavor pizza
