Tap Wagyo Burger

For the lovers of homemade burger, here is a easy recipe that anyone can prepare at home with simple ingredients that you can find on groceries close to your home.

Fr this recipe will be use 1kg of meat, what's blend 65% meat and 25% of fat. This blend you'll be able to prepare the burger in the plate or on the grill.

Let's do it!

  • 1kg of fresh meat
    • 750g of knuckle: I like to use knuckle, as it's low fat meat, than I can add just pure fat or a high fat meat, like brisket or ribs.
    • 250g of fat: Pay attention on the meat fat percentage to not add to much meat and reduce the fat %!
  • Fresh tomatoes
  • Pickles
  • Chedder chesse
  • Crispy lettuce
  • Onion if you prefer
  • Homemade mayonese
  • Burger buns
  • 250ml of fresh milk
  • 2-3 gloves of fresh garlic
  • Yellow mustard
  • Black pepper
  • salt
  • Oil - I prefer use oil as mutch neutral as possible, to not interfer on mayo flavor!
  • Blender machine

Directions

    1. Burger patties:
    • First thing first, miced the the meat using a ground machine or ask you butcher to miced for you.
    • After you get all your meat miced, it's time to blend very well all together.
    • Prepare the patties ball (better use a scale), I like to prepare patties with 150 - 170 g each, with this weight it's possible to adjust to point of the meat, middle well, well done, etc, according to your guesses tasty.
    • Now with all the patties balls ready, it's time to modeling the patties, if you have a 11cm form will be esiers, other wise, you can just modeling with your hands till get a thump finger thickness.
  2. Cut the buns in half and reserve
  3. Cut the tomatoes in slices, not to thin and not to thicker
  4. If you prefer cut the onios in slices as well
  5. Arrange the pickles, in case it's not cuted, slices them thiner.
    6. The Mayonese directions
    1. On the blender cup, add 250 ml of milk.
    2. a pinch of salt
    3. The fresh garlic
    4. 1 tsp of mustard
    5. A pinch of black pepper
    • Blend all those ingredients for at least 30sec, if is longer it's better. Here we're preparing the milk's protein to emulsify with the Oil.
    • Passing the 30sec, try it, to check if it's according to your flavor. At this time you can adjust the salt, black pepper, garlic and the mustard.
    • All good with your mayo base, it's time to incorporate the oil into the milk. Turn-on the blender and slowly add the oil. For this amount of milk, will need around 300 - 500ml of oil. That's will depend how thicker you like your mayonese.

So, at this point all your ingredients and base should be ready to start the BBQ time or just heat the fried pot to smell the burger aroma

All set up for your family or friend meal, it's time for grill the patties 3-5 min each side will be just good to get the right meat point.

  1. Harm up cutted buns
  2. Add one layer of mayo on the buns botton and crow
  3. On the buns botton, add the crispy lettuce
  4. Add the patty above the burger with melt chesee on it
  5. Then comes the tomato slice, some onions and pickles before close the top with the bun's crow.
  6. And voila, Oh just taste your juncy burger
