Popcorn Recipe

    Ingredients

  • General Oil
  • 50 g of corn for popcorn
  • Salt
  • A casserola pan with cover
  • A large bowl

Directions

  1. Add a drizzle of oil into the pan till cover the botton
  2. Turn the fire in medium to high heat
  3. Add the corns for popcorn
  4. Stir until the corn start to pop
  5. Close the pan with the cover
  6. Reduce a bit the heater
  7. Wait till the corns pop reduce
  8. Turn off the heat
  9. Immediatly tranfer the popcorn to the bowl.
  10. Add salt to taste.
  11. and voila, just start your favorty move and enjoy your popcorn.
